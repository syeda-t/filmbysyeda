About Me

Syeda S. Tabassum

I'm 17 years old, living in New York City. From an early age I was introudced to film,art,and photography through my oldest sister and my mom. I soon fell in love with being able to create my own art and to document my experiences. I originally began by taking portraits of my family members, I joined photography and yearbook club in middle school, and now I enjoy film making and editing as some of my favorite hobbies.
